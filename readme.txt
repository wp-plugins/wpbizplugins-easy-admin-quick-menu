The Easy Admin Quick Menu lets you add a simple quick menu in the admin panel, with big and visible buttons that your clients simply cannot miss.

== Description ==

Tired of your clients struggling to find their way around the WordPress admin panel? Tired of explaining where to click to add new users, blog posts, pages, or editing the site menus? Tired of teaching your clients where to find the most basic WordPress actions again and again? 
With this plugin you'll create a very visible and super-effective quick menu, making where to find the most common everyday WordPress actions blatantly obvious for your clients.

Ideal for working with clients who aren't technically interested enough to learn WordPress in depth, but needs to navigate efficiently to perform everyday things on their website.

* Make everyday work with the website less complex for your clients or administrators by adding big, visible buttons with shortcuts to the most common operations, like adding new blog posts, editing the site menu, editing pages, and more.
* Add descriptive icons, subtitles, titles and colors to your button to make it obvious and intuitive what the button does.
* Easily add menu buttons that link to *anything*, from any action in WordPress to external URLs, e-mails or similar.
* Minimal configuration needed, you're up and running right away.
* 100% mobile friendly. Finding your way through WordPress from your mobile device has never been easier.

**Instructions**

After you've installed and activated the plugin, you'll find the Easy Admin Quick Menu on the top of your admin dashboard. In the menu to your left, you'll find ways of adding, removing, editing and re-arranging menu items, as well as configuring the visual appearance of the menu.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working. Recommended way of installation is by searching for the plugin in the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress. You can also follow the instructions below:

1. Download the zip-file containing the plugin and extract it.
2. Upload the `wpbizplugins-easy-admin-quick-menu` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
4. Done!
